Kid-friendly Playlist

If you are a worried parent, and do not want to play a playlist containing “bad words”, this is the perfect feature for you! This feature allows you to insert up to three “bad words”, and builds a playlist containing 20 songs by the artist whose songs are most “kid friendly”
